Growth is a grind:

Constructive dissatisfaction:

Trust battery:

Vision and ground truth:
A good founder answer has both the future worth building and the uncomfortable facts right now.

Wartime versus peacetime:
Crisis requires faster calls and narrower priorities. Do not stay in wartime mode after the crisis passes.

Enterprise before team before self:
Optimize for the whole company before a function, team, or personal preference.

Culture is a second product:
A company creates one product for customers and another experience for employees.

Scale tax:
As companies grow, they accumulate complexity, slower decisions, layers, and local optimization. Leaders must simplify repeatedly.

MSPOT:
Mission, Strategy, Projects, Omissions, and Tracking. Omissions matter because focus requires visible no’s.
